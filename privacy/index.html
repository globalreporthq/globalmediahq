Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026 — Global Media Group

Global Media Group ("Company", "we", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information in connection with our services and tools.

1. Information We Collect

Our automated publishing tools may collect and process:

  • Platform authentication tokens (e.g., TikTok, X, Instagram OAuth credentials)
  • Content metadata (post text, media references, scheduling data)
  • System logs for performance and error monitoring

We do not collect personal information from end-users of published content.

2. How We Use Information

Information is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate and publish content to authorized platforms
  • Monitor system performance and diagnose errors
  • Improve our editorial pipelines

3. Third-Party Platforms

When our tools connect to third-party platforms such as TikTok, X, or Instagram, those platforms' own privacy policies govern data they collect. We only request the minimum permissions required to publish content on your behalf.

4. Data Retention

Authentication tokens and system logs are retained only as long as necessary for operational purposes and are not shared with third parties.

5. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect stored credentials and system data from unauthorized access.

6. Your Rights

You may revoke platform access at any time via the relevant platform's app settings (e.g., TikTok Developer Portal). Contact us to request deletion of any stored data.

7. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests: legal@globalmediahq.com
